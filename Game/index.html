Tutorial

Welcome to TriviaMania! In this game, your goal is to correctly answer the questions as fast as possible! The faster you are, the more points you get!

Questions can range from a variety of topics, from science, to history, to movies, and everything in between!

You have 4 choices to choose from, try your best to get it right before the timer runs out!

There is a total of 10 rounds for you to complete and achieve the highest score you can. Good luck!
